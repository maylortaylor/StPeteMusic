About Suite E

Suite E Studios is a DIY community music and creative space tucked inside St. Pete's Warehouse Arts District. Opened in 2025, we're artist-owned, artist-operated, and built on a philosophy of authenticity and collaboration.

No corporate feel, no gatekeeping.

Just a welcoming spot for musicians, artists, event workers, designers, multimedia producers, and a record shop. The warehouse feel is a feature, not a flaw.
