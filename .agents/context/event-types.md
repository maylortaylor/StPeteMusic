## General Promotion Format (Any Event)

All event posts should include:
1. **Who / What / When** ‚Äî clear at a glance
2. **Why It Matters** ‚Äî vibe, energy, why this event is worth attending
3. **Clear CTA** ‚Äî ticket link, doors time, "tag a friend"
4. **Date, time, cost, what to expect**
5. **Cross-branding:** Always include `@suite.e.studios` + `@stpetemusic` + `#StPeteMusic`
6. **Tag all performing artists/bands** ‚Äî always link their Instagram handles
